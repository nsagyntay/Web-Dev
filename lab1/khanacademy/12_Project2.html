Ramen

  • Time: 4 minutes
  • Serves: 1 people
Ingredient Quantity
Ramen 1
Water 3 cup
Egg 1

Steps:

1. Boil a water.

2. Heat a pan and open the ramen.

3. After 3 minutes add egg into pan.

Fried Chicken

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken 2
Salt To taste
Oil 1 tbsp

Steps:

1. Cut the chicken add salt.

2. Heat the oil in the pan.

3. Fry chicken.
